Part 1

Remote Work

The flexibility revolution — working from anywhere with digital-first collaboration.

Advantages

  • No commute saves 54 minutes daily
  • Better work-life balance
  • 16% productivity increase
  • Lower overhead costs
  • Access to global talent

Challenges

  • Increased isolation
  • Communication barriers
  • Home distractions
  • Tech setup burden
  • Fewer promotion opportunities
Part 2

Office Work

The traditional model — structured environments built for in-person collaboration.

Advantages

  • Face-to-face collaboration
  • Clear work-life boundaries
  • Better mentorship access
  • Immediate IT support
  • Stronger team culture

Challenges

  • Commute stress and time
  • Office interruptions
  • Higher costs ($20K/yr per seat)
  • Rigid schedules
  • Limited to local talent
