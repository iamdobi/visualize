✅ Pros & Cons

🏠 Remote Work

  • ✓ Flexible schedule & work-life balance
  • ✓ No commute — saves time & money
  • ✓ Access to global talent pool
  • ✓ Higher reported job satisfaction
  • ✗ Social isolation & loneliness
  • ✗ Blurred work-life boundaries
  • ✗ Harder to build team culture

🏢 Office Work

  • ✓ In-person collaboration & brainstorming
  • ✓ Stronger social bonds & mentorship
  • ✓ Clear separation: work vs home
  • ✓ Better onboarding for new hires
  • ✗ Costly commute & real estate
  • ✗ Less schedule flexibility
  • ✗ Open-office noise & distractions
