The Evolution of Artificial Intelligence

From Alan Turing's thought experiment to autonomous AI agents — 75 years of breakthroughs that reshaped our world.

75 Years of Progress
11 Key Milestones
2025 The Present
1950 🧠

The Turing Test

Alan Turing publishes "Computing Machinery and Intelligence," proposing the imitation game as a measure of machine thinking. This paper laid the philosophical foundation for the entire field of artificial intelligence.

1956 🎓

Dartmouth Conference

John McCarthy coins "artificial intelligence" at the Dartmouth Summer Research Project. This 8-week workshop brought together the founders who would define the field for decades to come.

1970s 💼

Expert Systems Era

Rule-based AI systems like MYCIN and DENDRAL prove that encoded human expertise can solve real-world problems. These systems demonstrated commercial viability and drove billions in corporate AI investment.

1987 ❄️

The AI Winter

Overpromised results and underwhelming expert systems trigger a massive funding collapse. Government and industry withdrew support, sending AI research into a decade-long chill.

1997 ♟️

Deep Blue Defeats Kasparov

IBM's Deep Blue becomes the first computer to defeat a reigning world chess champion under standard tournament conditions. The match captivated the world and reignited public fascination with AI.

2012 👁️

ImageNet & Deep Learning

AlexNet crushes the ImageNet competition with a deep convolutional neural network, slashing error rates nearly in half. This breakthrough proved that deep learning was the key to modern AI.

2016 🏆

AlphaGo Conquers Go

DeepMind's AlphaGo defeats world champion Lee Sedol in the ancient game of Go, long considered an impossible challenge for machines. It demonstrated that AI could master intuition-like reasoning.

2020 📝

GPT-3 Changes Everything

OpenAI's GPT-3 shows that scaling language models to 175 billion parameters produces emergent abilities in writing, coding, and reasoning. The era of large language models had begun.

2022 💬

ChatGPT Goes Viral

ChatGPT reaches 100 million users in just two months, becoming the fastest-growing consumer application in history. AI transformed overnight from a research curiosity to a mainstream tool.

2023 🚀

GPT-4 & Claude Arrive

Multimodal models like GPT-4 and Anthropic's Claude demonstrate near-human reasoning across text, code, and vision. The race toward artificial general intelligence accelerates dramatically.

2025 🤖

The Age of AI Agents

AI systems gain the ability to autonomously browse the web, write and execute code, and orchestrate complex multi-step tasks. The line between tool and collaborator begins to blur.

75 years of artificial intelligence — and the best is yet to come.
