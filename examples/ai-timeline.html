Early Foundations
1950

The Turing Test

Alan Turing publishes "Computing Machinery and Intelligence," proposing the fundamental question: can machines think?

Impact: Established the philosophical foundation for AI research and the first benchmark for machine intelligence.
1956

Birth of AI

The Dartmouth Summer Research Project coins "Artificial Intelligence." McCarthy, Minsky, and others formally launch AI as a field of study.

Impact: Created AI as a discipline and attracted the first wave of research funding.
1966

ELIZA Chatbot

Joseph Weizenbaum creates ELIZA, the first chatbot that engaged in conversation through pattern matching and substitution.

Impact: Demonstrated that simple algorithms could create convincing human-like interactions.
1980s

Expert Systems Boom

Systems like MYCIN and XCON demonstrate AI's commercial potential, generating billions in revenue across specialized domains.

Impact: First successful commercial AI applications, proving business value.
Strategic AI
1997

Deep Blue Defeats Kasparov

IBM's Deep Blue becomes the first computer to defeat a world chess champion, beating Garry Kasparov 3.5–2.5.

Impact: Proved AI could exceed human expertise in complex strategic domains.
Deep Learning Era
2006

Deep Learning Renaissance

Geoffrey Hinton's breakthroughs in deep neural networks, combined with GPU computing, make training large networks feasible.

Impact: Launched the modern AI era — vision, speech, and language understanding all advanced rapidly.
2012

ImageNet Breakthrough

AlexNet wins ImageNet with 15.3% error rate, dramatically outperforming traditional approaches at 26.2%.

Impact: Proved deep learning's superiority in computer vision, triggering massive AI investment.
2016

AlphaGo Masters Go

DeepMind's AlphaGo defeats world champion Lee Sedol 4–1 in Go, previously thought impossible for computers.

Impact: Showed AI could handle intuitive, creative thinking beyond brute-force calculation.
Generative AI
2020

GPT-3

OpenAI releases GPT-3 with 175B parameters, demonstrating unprecedented language understanding and generation.

Impact: Sparked the generative AI boom, leading to ChatGPT and widespread AI adoption.
2022

ChatGPT Goes Mainstream

ChatGPT reaches 100M users in 2 months — the fastest-growing consumer application in history.

Impact: Made AI accessible to everyone, transforming how people work, learn, and create.

The Next Frontier

AI continues evolving toward artificial general intelligence, with potential to revolutionize healthcare, education, scientific discovery, and creative expression.
