🏛️ Foundations
1950

Turing Test Proposed

Alan Turing publishes "Computing Machinery and Intelligence," posing the question: can machines think?

1958

The Perceptron

Frank Rosenblatt builds the Mark I Perceptron — the first neural network hardware, sparking hopes for machine learning.

🧠 Expert Systems
1970s

Expert Systems Era

Rule-based expert systems like MYCIN and DENDRAL bring AI to medicine and chemistry — the first commercial AI boom.

❄️ AI Winter
1987

AI Winter

Overpromise meets underdelivery. Funding collapses, the expert systems market crashes, and AI enters its cold decade.

♟️ Revival
1997

Deep Blue Beats Kasparov

IBM's Deep Blue defeats world chess champion Garry Kasparov, proving machines can master strategic games.

🔥 Deep Learning
2012

AlexNet & ImageNet

Deep convolutional neural network crushes the ImageNet competition, triggering the deep learning revolution.

2016

AlphaGo Beats Lee Sedol

DeepMind's AlphaGo defeats world Go champion, solving a game once thought decades away for AI.

2017

Transformer Architecture

Google publishes "Attention Is All You Need," introducing the architecture behind every modern LLM.

🚀 LLM Era
2020

GPT-3 Arrives

OpenAI's 175-billion-parameter model demonstrates remarkable language abilities, making "prompt engineering" a thing.

2022

ChatGPT Launches

OpenAI releases ChatGPT, reaching 100M users in 2 months — the fastest-growing consumer app in history.

2023

GPT-4 & Multimodal AI

GPT-4 brings vision, reasoning leaps, and near-human performance on professional exams. The multimodal era begins.

2025

AI Agents & Autonomy

Autonomous AI agents perform complex multi-step tasks, write code, and manage workflows independently.
