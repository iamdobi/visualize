# Round 40 Persona

**Role:** VP of Clinical Operations at a mid-size biotech company (Series C, 200 employees)
**Situation:** Preparing a board deck showing their drug pipeline status — 4 compounds in various trial phases, enrollment numbers, timelines, and budget burn rate. Board meeting is Thursday.
**Skill level:** Non-technical executive. Knows what a good presentation looks like (has seen McKinsey decks) but can't code. Wants something that looks like it came from an expensive agency.

## Prompts

1. "Create a drug pipeline dashboard showing 4 compounds across Phase 1-3 trials. Include enrollment progress bars, expected completion dates, and a budget burn chart. Make it look like something from a top-tier consulting firm."

2. "I need a timeline visualization showing our clinical trial milestones for 2024-2027. Four drug candidates: ABX-201 (oncology, Phase 3), ABX-305 (immunology, Phase 2), ABX-412 (rare disease, Phase 1), and ABX-518 (neurology, preclinical). Show key milestones like IND filing, first patient dosed, interim analysis, and expected NDA submission."

3. "Build a one-page executive summary infographic for our board. Key stats: $180M raised, 4 pipeline compounds, 12 active trial sites across 6 countries, 847 patients enrolled, 92% retention rate, 18 months average time to primary endpoint. Include a world map showing trial site locations and a pie chart of budget allocation across R&D (62%), G&A (18%), commercial prep (12%), and other (8%)."
