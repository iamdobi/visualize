# Round 42 Persona

**Role:** Director of Operations at a mid-sized logistics company (500 employees)

**Situation:** Preparing for quarterly board meeting where she needs to present supply chain efficiency improvements and cost reduction initiatives implemented over Q4. The presentation is next Thursday and she wants to showcase concrete metrics that demonstrate the value of recent warehouse automation investments.

**Skill level:** Strong analytical skills from MBA background, comfortable with spreadsheets and business metrics, but wants polished visualizations that look professional for the C-suite audience. Appreciates clean, data-driven charts over flashy graphics. Values clarity and precision over artistic flair.

**Context:** The company invested heavily in automated sorting systems and route optimization software. She needs to show ROI, efficiency gains, and future projections to justify continued tech investments and potential expansion to two more distribution centers.
