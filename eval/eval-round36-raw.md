## Top 5 Issues (Ranked by Impact)

1. **Inconsistent Theme Implementation** - Many files have dark mode that feels like an afterthought rather than an intentional design choice. Light themes often lack the polish of their dark counterparts.

2. **Limited Interactivity** - Most files feel static. Missing micro-interactions, subtle animations, and engaging hover states that make modern web experiences feel premium.

3. **Accessibility Gaps** - While basic accessibility is present, advanced features like screen reader optimization, keyboard navigation, and high contrast support are inconsistent.

4. **Mobile Polish** - While responsive, the mobile experience often feels like a shrunk desktop rather than a purposefully crafted mobile experience.

5. **Wow Factor Deficit** - Only 3-4 files reach the "viral" quality benchmark. Most feel professional but forgettable compared to Apple/Stripe standards.
